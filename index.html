AI over SMS Project

Bridging the Digital Divide

AI over SMS is a non-profit research initiative designed to provide offline access to AI-powered agricultural and educational information for communities with limited internet connectivity.

How the Service Works

Users can send text-based queries (e.g., "How to treat corn blight?") to our toll-free number and receive an AI-generated summary response. This service is intended for educational and research purposes only.

Mobile Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Program Name: AI over SMS Research Assistant

1. Opt-In Instructions

To start using the service, users must text the keyword START to +1 (866) 251-0758. By texting START, you explicitly consent to receive transactional AI responses to your queries from AI over SMS. Message frequency varies based on your user activity.

2. Opt-Out Instructions

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text STOP to +1 (866) 251-0758. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

3. Help & Support

If you are experiencing issues with the messaging program you can reply with the keyword HELP for more assistance, or you can get help directly at osali4@uic.edu.

4. Cost & Carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message and data rates may apply for any messages sent to you from us and to us from you.

5. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. We will use your phone number solely to provide the AI response service you requested. We do not share, sell, or disclose your phone number or opt-in data to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All data is used strictly for processing your immediate query.
