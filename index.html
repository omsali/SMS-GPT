AI over SMS

AI over SMS is a research project designed to bridge the digital divide. We provide AI-powered agricultural and informational assistance to users in rural areas via standard SMS, requiring no internet connection.

How it Works

Users can send a text message with their query (e.g., "How to cure tomato blight?") and receive a concise, AI-generated response.

How to Opt-In

To start using the service, text START to our toll-free number.

Support & Help

For help, text HELP at any time, or email us at [YOUR_EMAIL_HERE].

Opt-Out

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text STOP to the toll-free number. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. We do not share your phone number with third parties or affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Your number is used solely to provide the AI response service you requested.
