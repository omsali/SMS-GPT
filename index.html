Bringing AI to the Offline World

A non-profit research initiative bridging the digital divide by providing generative AI access via standard SMS protocols.

Our Services

Text-to-AI Query

Users send natural language questions via SMS and receive summarized, locally relevant answers generated by our AI engine.

Offline Accessibility

Designed for rural areas with no 4G/5G/Wi-Fi. Works on any basic feature phone with standard text messaging capabilities.

Educational Support

Provides instant access to agricultural data, educational content, and general knowledge without internet.

Mobile Terms of Service & Opt-In

Campaign Name: AI over SMS Research Project

1. How to Opt-In

To start using the service, text the keyword START to our Toll-Free Number +1 (866) 251-0758. By texting START, you explicitly consent to receive transactional AI responses to your specific queries. Message frequency varies based on your usage.

2. How to Opt-Out

Text STOP at any time to unsubscribe. We will send one final confirmation message, and then you will receive no further SMS.

3. Support

Text HELP for assistance or contact our support team at osali4@uic.edu.

4. Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan.

5. Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Phone numbers are collected solely for the purpose of delivering the requested AI responses. We do not share, sell, or disclose user data to third parties for marketing purposes.
